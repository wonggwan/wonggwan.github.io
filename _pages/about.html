--- permalink: / layout: archive title: "About me" excerpt: "About me" author_profile: true redirect_from: - /about/ - /about.html --- I am a fourth-year PhD candidate in Electrical Engineering at Washington University in St. Louis (WashU) advised by Prof. Yiannis Kantaros. Prior to my doctoral studies, I worked as a research intern at Schlumberger-Doll Research (SDR) for 9 months. I received MSE in Robotics at GRASP Lab supervised by Prof. George Pappas at the University of Pennsylvania in 2021 and BEng in Software Engineering at Sun Yat-Sen University in 2019. I spent 6 wonderful months at SungKyunKwan University in Korea as an exchange student in 2018.

My research interests include machine learning, control, and robotics.
